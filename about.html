About Us

Hello! We are a group of three seniors at Valley Regional Highschool in Deep River Connecticut. While us three our leading the fundraising efforts we are always working with Richard Naggot, a veteran himself and a very active member in the community as well as a few members of the Valley administration. Find out more about us Below!

Rinze Knoop

Hi, my name is Rinze Knoop. I am a senior at Valley Regional high school who is in the International Baccalaureate program. I am also Vice president and Treasurer of the National Honor Society. This WWII memorial is a part of my IB CAS project, and this topic was the one that popped out. I wanted to choose a project that positively impacted the community and replant the legacy of our fallen veterans.

Patrick Finnegan

Hi, my name is Patrick Finnegan. I’m 17 years old and a senior at Valley Regional High School, where I serve as class president and am a member of the National Honor Society. I come from a family of veterans, and I plan to follow in their footsteps by serving in the military. I wanted to participate in this project because it’s a meaningful way to honor not only the veterans in my own family but also the brave men from our community who sacrificed their lives in World War II.

Gavin Abderhalden

Hi, my name is Gavin Abderhalden. I am a senior at Valley Regional high school who is a member of the National Honor Society. I am a soccer team captain and a member of the baseball team, and I plan on going to a four year university next year. I am doing this project as part of my senior capstone, and I saw it as a great opportunity to positively contribute to our small town community and to continue to honor our fallen WWII veterans through new trees that will be at valley for generations to come.
